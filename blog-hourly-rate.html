How to Calculate Your Freelance Hourly Rate (Step-by-Step Guide)

Setting your freelance hourly rate can feel overwhelming. This guide walks you step-by-step through the process.

To calculate your rate, start with your desired annual income. Add your business expenses and tax percentage, then divide by the number of billable hours you expect to work. This ensures you cover costs and meet income goals.

Always review your rate every 6-12 months as your skills and expenses change.
