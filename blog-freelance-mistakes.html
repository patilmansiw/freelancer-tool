Top Mistakes Freelancers Make When Setting Rates

Many freelancers undercharge or miscalculate their pricing. Here are common mistakes and how to avoid them.

  • Forgetting to include taxes and expenses.
  • Comparing only to other freelancers without considering personal costs.
  • Not adjusting rates for experience growth.

Avoiding these mistakes will make your freelance career more sustainable.
