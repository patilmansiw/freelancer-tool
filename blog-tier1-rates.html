Why Freelancers in the US & UK Charge More (And How You Can Too)

Freelancers in Tier 1 countries often earn higher rates. Here’s why — and how you can apply the same principles.

Tier 1 freelancers charge more due to higher living costs, strong demand, and market expectations. To compete, focus on specialization, building trust with clients, and delivering premium service.

Even if you’re outside Tier 1, adopting these strategies can justify higher rates.
