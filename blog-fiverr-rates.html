Average Fiverr Rates by Profession (2025 Update)

We analyzed guidance from Fiverr and common marketplace pricing to summarize typical ranges by category. Use these as a reference; your final rate should reflect your experience, niche, demand, and deliverables.

Typical Fiverr Ranges (2025)

  • Web Designer: $30–$200/hr
  • Graphic Designer: $25–$180/hr (logos often $55–$125 per project)
  • Content Writer: ~ $23/hr (varies by niche & length)
  • SEO Expert: $50–$200/hr
  • Digital Marketer: $25–$230+/hr

Fiverr vs Tier‑1 Market Comparison
